About the Collaboration Server

Our Python libraries were updated on 18 October 2024. View changelogs for the icoscp and icoscp_stilt libraries for more information.

Our collaborative Jupyter Hub service is for registered users and offers advanced options like:

  • sharing notebooks and data
  • uploading ancillary data for analysis together with ICOS data
  • permanent storage of notebooks and data

Please visit the Jupyter Notebook page on our website to learn more about our collaborative service, view documentation, and apply for access to either this service or our Exploredata service.

To ensure you are using the latest version of our plugins, you can restart your Jupyter instance.
